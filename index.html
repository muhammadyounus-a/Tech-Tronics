Products & Services

Laboratory Equipment & Instruments

Our extensive range of laboratory equipment and instruments is designed to meet the diverse needs of the scientific community. From precision balances and scales to advanced centrifuges, incubators, and microscopes, our products are known for their durability and performance. We work closely with reputable manufacturers to ensure our offerings meet international standards and facilitate cutting-edge research and diagnostics.

Variety of Chemicals

We supply a comprehensive range of chemicals, including GC-MS/LC-MS Grade, ACS Reagent Grade, HPLC Grade, and AR Grade chemicals. Whether you're in the research, healthcare, or education sector, our wide array of specialty chemicals, microbiological media, and prepared petri plates ensures you get the precise solutions you need for your work.

Plasticwares & Glasswares

Our plastic and glassware products are essential for any laboratory setup. From test tubes, beakers, and pipettes to volumetric flasks and filtration assemblies, our products provide the precision and quality needed for accurate scientific results. Our range includes both sterile and non-sterile options to accommodate various laboratory requirements.

Specialized Laboratory Furniture & Safety Products

Techtronic LLC offers a wide range of specialized laboratory furniture, including workstations, storage cabinets, and customized lab tables. In addition, we provide essential lab safety products, such as eye wash stations, spill kits, chemical-resistant gloves, and first aid kits, ensuring a safe and efficient work environment.

Medical Instruments & Furnitures

Our portfolio of medical instruments and furniture is tailored for healthcare providers. We offer everything from patient monitors, ECG machines, and defibrillators to hospital beds, medication carts, and examination couches. Our medical products are designed to meet the stringent demands of modern healthcare facilities, ensuring both patient comfort and operational efficiency.

Educational Resources

We are dedicated to supporting educational institutions with high-quality science lab equipment, chemistry apparatus, and biology models. Our comprehensive range includes everything from basic measuring tools to advanced microscopes and dissection sets, enabling schools and universities to offer hands-on learning experiences to their students.
